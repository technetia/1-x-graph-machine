Functions II

Trigonometric

NOTE: Arguments are in radians.

-- sin(x), cos(x), tan(x): sine, cosine, tangent of x, respectively
-- asin(x), acos(x), atan(x): inverses of the above, respectively

-- csc(x), sec(x), cot(x): cosecant, secant, cotangent of x, respectively
-- acsc(x), asec(x), acot(x): inverses of the above, respectively

Hyperbolic
-- sinh(x), cosh(x), tanh(x): hyperbolic sine, cosine, tangent of x, respectively
-- asinh(x), acosh(x), atanh(x): inverses of the above, respectively

-- csch(x), sech(x), coth(x): hyperbolic cosecant, secant, cotangent of x, respectively
-- acsch(x), asech(x), acoth(x): inverses of the above, respectively
